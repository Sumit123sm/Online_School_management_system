SCHOOL FEES


Entrance Fees is the same as the monthly Fee.

(1) Fees should be paid on or before 15t of each month otherwise pupils may not be admitted into the class and be liable to a fine.
(2) FEES FOR THE MONTHS OF APRIL AND MAY ARE TO BE PAID IN APRIL BEFORE THE PUPILS APPEAR FOR THE ANNUAL EXAMINATION
(3) Pupils who have not paid their fees will not be allowed to appear for examination.
4) Junior students should not be entrusted with the payment of fees.
5) Fees will be received daily. Amount once paid will not refundable in any case whatever.
(6) H. S. C. & S. S. C. Students will have to clear their fees up to May by December.
(7) In Secondary and Junior College students belonging to B.C., E.B.C., N.T, S.T. and O.B.C. will be granted concession according to the Government Rules. To avail of this facility the prescribed form should be obtained and submitted to the office before the stipulated date. Incomplete forms will not be accepted
(8) Fee concession will be granted only to those students who are good in studies and in discipline.
(9) THE PARENTS SHOULD NOTE THAT FOURTH CHILD IS NOT ENTITLED FOR ANY KIND OF FREESHIPS
(10) All the students are granted free ships, if,
(Î) They should produce a certificate of residence in Maharashtra.
(ii) The students should have passed the previous classes.
(iii)The students should not be the 4th child.

ADMISSION


1. Each pupil seeking admission to the school should produce a Leaving Certificate if he/she comes from a recognized school and if from States other than Maharashtra the Leaving Certificate should be duly countersigned by the Educational Authority of the Particular District.
2. All the students who have passed Sr. KG. and want to take admission in Std. I and Std. IV to S td. V should take re-admission.
3. A pupil, who seeks admission to the school for the first time, will have to produce his/her Birth Certificate issued by a Competent Authority.
4. Pupil who completes 5 years on 31s July can be admitted to standard I (and progressively to other classes). Each pupil at the same time of admission will have to pay an admission fees equivalent to the monthly fee. In Junior College no admission will be given on Duplicate L. C.



WITHDRAWALS


Application for the Leaving Certificate should be submitted in the prescribed form which is attached to the calendar duly signed by the Parent/Guardian and applicant should call at the School Office after a week's time or send a self-addressed and stamped envelope if the same is to be posted.
Fee for the whole term will have paid if a pupil joins or leaves the school during the term.

First Term : June to October


Second Term : November to May



LEAVE OF ABSENCE



1. No pupil will be allowed to be absent without obtaining leave in advance
2. If a pupil is late or absent, he/she must bring a written explanation in the 'Absence Record' in his/her Calendar. The School disclaims all responsibilities if a boy/girl is obliged to return home during school hours
3. Parents/Guardians who wish to detain their children at home for any reason should apply for Leave in the prescribed `Leave Record in the Calendar as well as produce a Leave Letter on fool'scap paper as per the draft provided in the calendar for School record
4. In case of long leave, the class teacher should be informed beforehand and a permission to be sought.
5. Truancy must be checked by Parents / Guardians / Teachers 6. Names of pupils who have been absent for more than 3 months without permission will be struck off the roll and as a rule they will not be re-admitted
7. Pupils must return after the holidays on the reopening day. In case of sickness the school authorities must be notified and a Doctor's Certificate should be produced along with application on fools'cap paper as per the draft provided in the calendar.
8. Holidays will be granted as prescribed for educational Institution by the Government of Maharashtra

EXAMINATION


1. Two Unit Tests/Formative Examinations and Two Semester Examinations/Summative Examinations will be held in the course of the year.
2. Pupils absenting from the examinations without grave reasons will be considered as having failed. Pupils absenting from any examination including P. T. will not be re examined
3. In order to be eligible for appearing for the Second Semester Examination, the pupils.
Must have paid all fees and dues by the 15th of March. Must have returned all the books taken from the Book Bank and School Library. Must have done the extra reading prescribed for the standards Must not have remained absent for more than 25% Of the number of working days during the year. Must have preserved the calendar with all details filled therein No text books, note books or other papers are to be brought into the Examination Hall. A pupil not carrying out the instruction given by the supervisor will be liable to be expelled from the Examination Hall.
