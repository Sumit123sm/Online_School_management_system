Guidelines for Admission



Parents/Guardians seeking admission to their ward should complete an online application form. Admissions for the new academic year will begin in January from Kindergarten to IX STD.

Please contact the school office for guidance on filling online application form from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. at these Phone Nos: 044-22452253, 044-22448535, 8925503661

Selection Procedure



The academic council will scrutinize each application based on the data and details furnished in the application form and offer admission to the child to the appropriate standard.

New admission will be finalized - based on the following:

General Procedure



  • Availability of seats / Vacancies
  • Priority in the order of registration
  • Academic performance in the previous school

    • Specific Procedure



  • LKG & UKG - personal interview with child and parents.
  • I to IX - performance in the Entrance Examination followed by Personal Interview of the student and parent of the shortlisted candidates.
  • XI Class - Marks scored in the X class Board Examination will be considered.

    • Criteria for admissions



    Age criteria for LKG, UKG, and class I

  • LKG: The child must complete 3 years as of 31st May 2024 (Born between 1.8.2020 to 31.7.2021)
  • UKG: The child must complete 4 years as of 31st May 2024 (Born between 1.8.2019 to 31.7.2020)
  • Class I: The child must complete 5 years as of 31st May 2024 (Born between 1.8.2018 to 31.7.2019)

    • Necessary documents for admission



    Photocopies of the following :
  • Student's Birth Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card (Student's, Student's Father/guardian, and Student's Mother) - If applied acknowledgement receipt
  • Community Certificate
  • Passport size photo of student Migration Certificate (applicable for Student studied in the board other than Tamil Nadu State Board)
