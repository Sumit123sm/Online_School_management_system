WELCOME TO

Prasad English High School, Mumbai

Prasad VIDAYAK SOCIETY established Prasad ENGLISH HIGH SCHOOL in the year 1999 situated at Mumbai East in Shere Punjab Society. We provide quality education, right from the Pre Primary to the Secondary school Certificate (SSC)

Prasad English High School is an institution with students having special talents. We nurture each child’s unique set of talents, strength, and skills through a balanced mind, body and soul programme. We teach them to become independent, be kind to their friends and cherish and respect each and everyone.


Join us in making this dream a reality!
