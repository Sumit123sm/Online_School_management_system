Events



The Institute is equipped with sufficient sports facilities for several indoor and outdoor games for students and staff. Indoor games include Table-Tennis, Carom and Chess and the outdoor games include Badminton, Cricket, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, kaddabi, Kho-Kho, Throwball etc. In addition Multi-gym facilities are also available for all the students and staff. The Sports equipments / kits are issued to the students for both practice sessions as well as during tournaments. The institute regularly organizes the Inter branch and inter hostel Sports competition for (Boys & Girls) and Cultural events. Besides organizing institute also participate in All India Inter NIT Sports Meet in various events.
